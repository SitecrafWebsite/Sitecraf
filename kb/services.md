# Sitecraf Services

## Business Websites

Sitecraf builds professional business websites on WordPress, Next.js, or Wix Studio — whichever platform best fits your growth stage. Every website is mobile-first and SEO-ready from day one, with contact forms and WhatsApp buttons built in. Most projects go live in as little as 2 weeks. Suitable for service businesses, agencies, blogs, and portfolio sites.

## Landing Pages

Landing pages at Sitecraf are built with conversion-led architecture — every scroll, click, and form field is optimised to turn ad spend into leads. They are A/B test ready and built to Core Web Vitals standards for fast load times. Ideal for businesses running paid ads on Google or Meta who need a dedicated high-performance page.

## E-Commerce Stores

Sitecraf builds Shopify stores and WooCommerce setups for D2C brands and product sellers. Every store includes full product catalogue setup, payment and shipping configuration, and an optimised checkout flow to reduce drop-offs. Multi-currency and international selling are supported. WhatsApp sales support integration is available as an add-on.

## AI Chatbots

Sitecraf builds AI chatbots trained on your specific business — your products, pricing, and FAQs. The chatbot handles customer enquiries 24/7, captures leads when you're offline, and supports Hindi, Hinglish, and English. It includes a website widget (one-line embed), WhatsApp handoff, and can sync captured leads to a CRM or Google Sheets. Up to 50 Q&A pairs are included in the initial training.

## Workflow Automation

Sitecraf connects your website to WhatsApp, CRM systems, Google Sheets, payment gateways, and booking calendars. Automations cover lead notification workflows, form-to-CRM pipelines, Razorpay/Stripe payment setup, and calendar integrations. Every automation is tested and documented before handover. Priced per integration as an add-on to any project.

## AI Image Generation

Sitecraf produces studio-quality product photos, lifestyle visuals, and banners from your raw images — no photographer required. Each batch delivers 30–50 images with lifestyle backgrounds, room settings, and on-brand styling throughout. Files are exported in web-optimised WebP and JPG formats. Bulk batches attract a discount, and 2 style revisions are included per batch.
