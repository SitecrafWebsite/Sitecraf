# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I know you won't take my advance and go silent?

Work starts with a 50% advance, and the remaining payment is due only after you have reviewed and approved the completed website — you see the site before Sitecraf sees the final payment. A fixed delivery timeline is given in writing before work begins, not a vague estimate. This structure protects clients from the common horror story of advances disappearing with no delivery.

## What do I need to provide before you can start building?

Three things: your business details (what you do, who you serve, your location), any logos or brand assets you have (even a rough one is fine), and your content or a rough idea of what pages you need. You do not need a written brief, a wireframe, or prior website experience. Sitecraf asks a structured set of questions during onboarding that pulls out everything required — most clients are surprised by how little effort is needed from their side.

## Is the 2–3 week timeline realistic or just marketing?

It is realistic with one condition: the timeline depends on how quickly you review and give feedback. Sitecraf's build side is fast; the main delay in most projects is waiting on client approvals. If you respond within 24–48 hours at each stage, 2–3 weeks is very achievable — the fastest project launched in under 12 days. Projects with slow feedback have stretched to 6 weeks. The timeline is a shared responsibility.

## How much does a website cost?

WordPress websites start from ₹6,000. Wix Studio websites start from ₹10,000. Shopify stores start from ₹15,000. Custom Next.js websites start from ₹15,000. AI chatbot add-on starts from ₹12,000. AI-generated product images start from ₹3,000 per batch. All prices are fixed and quoted in writing before work begins — no surprise invoices, no GST added on top. Most small business websites land between ₹8,000–₹25,000.

## What is included in the price and what costs extra?

Every project includes design, development, mobile responsiveness, basic SEO setup, and two weeks of post-launch support. What costs extra: copywriting (available separately), domain registration, hosting subscription fees for platforms like Shopify or Wix that charge monthly, and add-ons like AI chatbot or image generation. Every extra cost is disclosed before work starts — nothing is added silently mid-project.

## I am not technical. Can I update the website myself after it launches?

Yes — this is specifically designed for. On WordPress and Wix Studio, you can edit text, swap images, add blog posts, and update prices without touching any code. After launch, Sitecraf records a short walkthrough video of your specific website showing you how to make the most common changes. For custom Next.js builds, minor content updates are handled by Sitecraf, or a headless CMS can be set up so your team can self-manage.

## What are SEO, AEO, and GEO and why does my business need them?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps your website rank in Google search results. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) helps AI tools like Perplexity and Google AI Overviews cite your business as a direct answer to user questions. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) helps ChatGPT, Gemini, and Copilot mention your business when users ask for recommendations. Search behaviour is splitting across all three channels — Google alone is no longer enough. Sitecraf builds AEO and GEO-ready structure into every website including schema markup, Q&A content, entity signals, and fast Core Web Vitals.

## What happens if something breaks after the website goes live?

Two weeks of post-launch support is included in every project at no extra charge. If something breaks — a form stops working, a page looks wrong on a device, an image does not load — it is fixed with no questions asked within that window. After two weeks, Sitecraf is still available for changes and fixes on a per-task basis. For businesses that want ongoing maintenance, monthly retainer packages are available covering security updates, content changes, and performance monitoring.

## Is the 50% upfront payment refundable?

The upfront payment is used to begin work immediately — research, design, and setup. If you cancel before any design files or previews are delivered, 75% of the upfront is refunded. After design approval, the deposit is non-refundable. The second 50% is never asked for until you have seen and approved the finished work.

## Do you charge GST?

No. Sitecraf is not GST-registered, so there is no GST added to your invoice. The price in your quote is exactly what you pay — nothing more.

## What payment methods do you accept?

UPI, bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS), Razorpay (cards, net banking, wallets), and international payments via Wise or Payoneer. Cash is not accepted. International clients can pay in USD, GBP, EUR, or AED at the live exchange rate — no hidden forex markup.

Yes. If you order a website plus AI chatbot together, or a website plus AI images together, Sitecraf offers a bundled discount of ₹2,000–₹5,000 depending on the combination. Ask on WhatsApp before placing your order to have the discount applied to your quote.
