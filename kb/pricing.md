# Sitecraf Pricing

## WordPress Website

Starting from ₹6,000 (one-time). Delivery in 2–3 weeks. Includes up to 4 pages, mobile-first SEO-ready build, easy CMS so your team can update content, contact form and WhatsApp button, 2 revision rounds, and 2 weeks of post-launch support. All logins and intellectual property are handed over on final payment. Best for service businesses, agencies, blogs, and SEO-driven brands.

## Shopify Store

Starting from ₹15,000 (one-time setup fee). Delivery in 2–4 weeks. Includes full product catalogue setup, payment and shipping configuration, optimised checkout to reduce drop-offs, multi-currency support, 2 revision rounds, and 2 weeks of post-launch support. The Shopify platform subscription (approximately ₹2,000/mo) is paid directly to Shopify by you.

## Wix Studio Website

Starting from ₹10,000 (one-time). Delivery in 2–3 weeks. Includes a visual builder you can edit yourself without a developer, contact form and booking integration, professional design, 2 revision rounds, and 2 weeks of post-launch support. The Wix platform plan (₹450/mo) is paid directly to Wix by you. Best for local businesses, clinics, restaurants, and studios.

## Custom Next.js Website

Starting from ₹15,000 (one-time). Delivery in 3–5 weeks. Includes up to 10 pages built from scratch (no templates), near-perfect Google PageSpeed scores, free hosting on Vercel forever, SEO metadata and sitemap, and 100% code ownership transferred to you. Best for performance-critical sites and custom web apps with no platform lock-in.

## AI Chatbot

Starting from ₹12,000 (one-time setup). Delivery in 1–3 weeks. Includes training on your business data (up to 50 Q&A pairs), Hindi/Hinglish/English support, lead capture and qualification, a website widget (one-line embed), and WhatsApp handoff integration. Hosting costs vary depending on traffic. CRM integration is available as an add-on for ₹8,000 more.

## AI Image Generation

₹3,000 per batch of 30–50 images. Delivered in 3–5 days. Includes lifestyle backgrounds and room settings, brand-consistent styling throughout, web-optimised WebP and JPG exports, and 2 style revisions per batch. Bulk batches attract a discount. Does not include physical photography, videography, or logo/branding design.

## Automation and Integrations

₹8,000 per integration (add-on to any project). Delivered in 3–7 days. Covers WhatsApp and email notifications, Google Sheets or CRM sync, Razorpay/Stripe payment setup, calendar and booking integrations, form-to-CRM automation, plus testing and documentation. Monthly API costs from third parties (e.g. WhatsApp Business API) are paid separately by the client.

## Monthly Retainers

The **Monthly Care Plan** is ₹3,500/mo and includes up to 4 hours of changes per month, priority WhatsApp support, a monthly performance report, and minor content and image updates. The **Growth Retainer** is ₹8,000/mo and includes up to 10 hours of development per month, new pages and feature additions, SEO updates and blog publishing, chatbot retraining as needed, and a 30-minute monthly strategy call. Both plans can be paused or cancelled with 15 days' notice — no long-term lock-in.

## What Is Always Included

Every Sitecraf project includes: a fixed written quote before work begins (no hourly billing), zero hidden charges, a 50% advance with the remaining 50% due only after you approve the completed work, 100% intellectual property ownership transferred to you, 2 weeks of free post-launch support, and no GST charged. These are non-negotiable across every service and price point.

## Payment Terms

Payment is split 50% upfront to start work and 50% after you have reviewed and approved the finished project. Accepted methods: UPI, bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS), Razorpay (cards, net banking, wallets), and international payments via Wise or Payoneer. No GST is added — Sitecraf is not GST-registered. International clients can pay in USD, GBP, EUR, or AED at the live exchange rate with no forex markup.

## Bundle Discounts

If you order a website together with an AI chatbot, or a website together with AI image generation, Sitecraf offers a bundled discount of ₹2,000–₹5,000 depending on the combination. Ask via WhatsApp before placing your order to get the bundled rate applied. The upfront payment (50%) is refundable at 75% if you cancel before any design files or previews are delivered; after design approval, the deposit is non-refundable.
