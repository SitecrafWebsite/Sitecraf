# How Sitecraf Works: From Inquiry to Launch

## Step 1: Discovery and Brief

Reach out via WhatsApp (+91 9599143235), the contact form on the website, or email (info@sitecraf.com). You only need three things to get started: your business details (what you do, who you serve, your location), any logos or brand assets you have (even a rough one is fine), and a rough idea of what pages you need. Sitecraf sends a structured onboarding questionnaire that pulls out everything else — no written brief or prior website experience required.

## Step 2: Strategy and Platform Selection

Before any design work begins, Sitecraf focuses on understanding your customers, goals, and buyer psychology. Based on your growth stage and requirements, a platform is recommended: WordPress for SEO-driven content sites, Shopify for e-commerce, Wix Studio for fast-launch local businesses, or custom Next.js for performance-critical builds. You receive a fixed written quote based on a clear scope before work begins — no hourly billing, no surprises.

## Step 3: Design and Development

The build is mobile-first by default, with SEO, AEO (Answer Engine Optimization), and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) structure baked in from the start. Schema markup, Q&A content, entity signals, and Core Web Vitals optimisation are included in every build. Sitecraf targets sub-2-second load times and near-zero hosting costs wherever possible. You will not be asked for the final 50% payment until the work is ready for your review.

## Step 4: Review and Revisions

Every project includes 2 revision rounds. Once the initial build is ready, you review it and submit feedback. Sitecraf asks you to respond within 24–48 hours at each stage to keep the project on schedule. The 2–3 week timeline is a shared responsibility — projects where clients are responsive have launched in under 12 days; slow feedback can stretch timelines to 6 weeks.

## Step 5: Launch and Handover

After you approve the final version and make the remaining 50% payment, the site goes live. Every login, file, and line of code is transferred to you — full IP ownership, no lock-in. For WordPress and Wix Studio builds, a short walkthrough video is recorded showing you how to make common updates yourself. Two weeks of free post-launch support begins from the launch date.

## Timeline Expectations

Typical delivery is 2–3 weeks. The fastest project was completed in under 12 days with a highly responsive client. AI chatbot and automation add-ons can be delivered in 1–7 days depending on complexity. Custom Next.js builds take 3–5 weeks. The main variable in any timeline is how quickly the client reviews and approves work at each stage — Sitecraf's build side is consistently fast.
